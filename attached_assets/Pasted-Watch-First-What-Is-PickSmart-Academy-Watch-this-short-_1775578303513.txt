The Real Talk

New to Order Selecting? Here's what nobody tells you.
Order selecting is one of the most physically demanding — and highest-paying — entry-level warehouse jobs. But most new hires are thrown in with zero training.

Why most people struggle
Voice-directed systems, heavy cases, time pressure, and pallet building all hit at once on day one.

What top selectors know
It is not about being the strongest. It is about route planning, pallet technique, and mental pacing.

How this course bridges the gap
We break everything down into simple, actionable steps so you build confidence before you hit the floor.
