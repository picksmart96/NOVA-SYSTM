# PickSmart NOVA — User Handbook

---

## What Is PickSmart NOVA?

PickSmart NOVA is a warehouse training platform built for grocery selectors and warehouse teams. It combines voice-directed picking simulation, AI coaching, team dashboards, and training modules — all in one place.

---

## 1. Getting Access

### Step 1 — Visit the Site
Go to the website. The **Home page** and **Pricing page** are open to everyone — no account needed.

### Step 2 — Choose a Plan
Click **View Plans** or go to the **Pricing** page. There are two plans:

| Plan | Best For | What's Included |
|---|---|---|
| **Personal** | Individual selectors | Training, NOVA Help, NOVA Trainer, Common Mistakes, Leaderboard, Selector Nation |
| **Company** | Warehouses and teams | Everything in Personal + Trainer Dashboard + Supervisor Dashboard + Team tools |

Click **Choose Personal** or **Choose Company** to continue.

### Step 3 — Choose Your Plan Page
Confirm your plan choice. Click **Continue Personal** or **Continue Company**.

### Step 4 — Checkout
Review what's included in your plan and click:
- **Complete Personal Subscription** — for Personal
- **Complete Company Subscription** — for Company

> You must be signed in to complete checkout. If you aren't signed in yet, click **Sign in** first.

## 2. Signing In

1. Click **Sign in** in the top-right corner (or go to `/login`)
2. Enter your **username** and **password**
3. Click **Sign in**

> Every time you close the browser or refresh the page, you will need to sign in again. This is by design for security.

## 5. NOVA Help — AI Voice Coach

**Path:** NOVA Help (in the menu)

NOVA Help is your personal AI coach. Ask it anything about picking, warehouse operations, building techniques, or voice commands.

**How to use:**
1. Click the microphone button and speak your question — or type it in the text box
2. NOVA Help will answer in English or Spanish (it detects your language automatically)
3. Ask follow-up questions just like a conversation

**Example questions:**
- "What aisle is slot 25 in?"
- "How do I build a stable pallet?"
- "What does NOVA say when I confirm a pick?"
- "¿Cómo confirmo un pick?" *(Spanish — works too)*

## 6. NOVA Trainer — Voice Simulation

**Path:** NOVA Trainer (in the menu) — Company plan only

NOVA Trainer simulates the ES3 voice-directed picking system used in real warehouses. It walks you through a full picking session using real commands and responses.

### How to Start
1. Go to **NOVA Trainer**
2. Enter your **NOVA ID** (e.g., NOVA-25917) or select from the registered selectors list
3. Click **Begin Session**
4. The session starts automatically — NOVA will speak to you

### What Happens During a Session
NOVA guides you through each step in order:

| Step | What NOVA Says / Expects |
|---|---|
| Sign On | NOVA asks for your equipment number |
| Confirm Equipment | Confirm the number NOVA repeats |
| Max Pallet Count | State how many pallets you can hold |
| Safety Check | Answer 9 safety questions |
| Load Picks | Say "load picks" when ready |
| Pick Summary | NOVA reads your assignments |
| Setup Alpha / Bravo | Confirm your staging doors |
| Pick Check | NOVA gives slot and quantity to pick |
| Pick Ready | Say "ready" when the pick is complete |
| Complete | NOVA closes out each door |

### Voice Commands You'll Use
| What to Say | When |
|---|---|
| Your equipment number (e.g., "zero one") | When asked for equipment |
| "Correct" or "yes" | To confirm |
| "No" or "incorrect" | To deny / repeat |
| "Load picks" | When picks are ready to load |
| "Ready" | When a pick is complete |
| "NOVA" | Wake word (if not in auto mode) |
| "Stop" | End the session |

### Manual Input
If your microphone isn't working, use the text box at the bottom to type commands and click **Send**.

### Exiting a Session
Click the **Exit** button. This will lock your session — you'll need your password to resume, or you can sign out instead.

## 11. Session Lock

While logged in, you can lock your session so no one else can use it while you step away.

**To lock:**
- Click your avatar (top-right) → **🔒 Lock session**
- Or: Exit a NOVA Trainer session

**To unlock:**
- Enter your password on the lock screen
- Click **Unlock**

**If you can't remember your password:**
- Click **Sign out instead** on the lock screen

## 15. For the Owner (Admin)

The owner account has full access to everything, including:

### Control Center (`/owner`)
- **Stats** — Total subscribers, active users today, total sessions, banned users
- **User Management** — Search, filter, change roles, ban/unban, remove accounts
- **Invite Management** — Generate invite links by role (selector, trainer, supervisor)
- **Plan Control** — Manage Personal and Company plan visibility
- **Admin Movement Log** — Audit log of all actions taken on the platform

### Users & Access (`/users-access`)
- Create and manage user accounts directly
- View pending invites
- Manage NOVA access and active accounts

> These pages are **only visible to the owner account.** No other user — including Company subscribers — can see or access them.

---

## 16. Inviting Users

Only the owner can invite new users.

1. Go to **Control Center** → **Invite Management**
2. Enter the person's **full name** and **email**
3. Select their **role** (Selector, Trainer, or Supervisor)
4. Click **Generate Invite Link**
5. Copy the link and send it to them

The invited person clicks the link, sets a username and password, and their account is created.
